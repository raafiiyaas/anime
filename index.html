DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer is a breathtaking action-fantasy anime following Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. With stunning animation from Ufotable and an emotionally gripping story, Demon Slayer has become one of the most popular anime worldwide.
